Computer Science Graduates in Need of Help!

Computer Science graduates are facing difficulties in getting a job in their fields

The Issues

No Experience

CS graduates struggle in obtaining a job since they do not have the work experience required by companies.

No Practical Skills

CS graduates do not have the skills to perform the tasks that companies are in need of. Skills like development frameworks or cloud computing.

Weak Communication

CS graduates are having trouble with communicating effectively which is hindering them in interviews and networking.

The Solutions

Develop Your Skills

Start to acquire the skills needed by companies and do outside projects to showcase what you have learned and add to your resume!

Network with Others

Connect with fellow graduates and professionals in the field to receive potential referrals and advice for getting a job offer!

Stay Determined

Do not give Up! Getting a job can be a challenging process however keep on working towards building your tehcnical skills and networking
